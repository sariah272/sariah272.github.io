The price of success is hard work, dedication to the work, and determining whether we win or lose, we have exerted our best abilities for the work.

No success is gained instantly. There is no one who sells success. The only person who can give you success is yourself. Not with the money and treasures you have, you can only get it when you really work hard and dedicate yourself and even live for the goal you want to achieve.

Be yourself, express yourself, rest assured of yourself, do not go and look for a successful personality and duplicate it.

There has never been a story of a successful person without a struggle. Behind people's success, there are their stories when trying to make their lives better every day. Not infrequently we encounter the stories of those who always try with all their might, dedicating their lives to work as hard and smart as possible so that their dreams are realized. Not infrequently they also experience failures in their lives. But they never gave up and even continued to do the same struggle
